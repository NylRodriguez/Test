Coraline

Coraline is a dark fantasy animated film based on Neil Gaiman's novel. It tells the story of a young girl who discovers a parallel universe that appears better than her own — but hides sinister secrets beneath its surface.

Key Information

  • Genre: Dark Fantasy, Animation, Adventure
  • Release Year: 2009
  • Director: Henry Selick
  • Runtime: 100 minutes

Cast & Creative Team

Directed by Henry Selick and produced by Laika Studios.

  • Dakota Fanning as Coraline Jones (voice)
  • Teri Hatcher as Mel Jones / Other Mother (voice)
  • Keith David as Charlie Jones (voice)
  • Jennifer Saunders as Miss Spink (voice)
  • Ian McShane as Mr. Bobinsky (voice)

Main Characters

  • Coraline Jones – adventurous young girl
  • Other Mother – sinister alternate version of Coraline's mom
  • Wybie Lovat – Coraline's quirky friend
  • Mr. Bobinsky – eccentric neighbor and mouse circus owner
  • Miss Spink and Miss Forcible – retired actresses and neighbors

Reviews
