Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by braising chicken in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. It's savory, tangy, and delicious with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp oil (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water

Method

  1. In a pot, combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  2. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil. Then lower the heat and simmer for 30â€“40 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  4. Optional: In a separate pan, heat oil and lightly fry the chicken pieces until browned. Return to sauce and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
